About the Office

The Mayor of Calgary is the chief elected official of the city of Calgary, Alberta, Canada. The mayor is elected at-large to a four-year term and serves as the chair of Calgary City Council.

Key Responsibilities

  • Providing leadership and vision for the city
  • Chairing City Council meetings
  • Representing the city at official functions
  • Working with council to set priorities and policies
  • Engaging with citizens and community groups
