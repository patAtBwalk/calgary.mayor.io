Meet Jordan

Civil engineer → tech founder → community volunteer. Jordan grew up in Calgary (child of a welder and a nurse), studied engineering at the U of C, built municipal projects from water systems to LRT upgrades, then co‑founded a startup that modernized permitting software. He sold the company and now helps design affordable housing. He lives in Hillhurst with two kids who commandeer his toolbox.

Leadership style: pragmatic empathy, clear targets, public dashboards. If it can’t be measured, it probably can’t be managed.
